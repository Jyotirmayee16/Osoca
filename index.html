OUR CERTIFICATION: ASSURANCE FOR
YOUR COMMITMENTS

OSOCA proposes to provide an institutional mechanism for Organic Inspection and Certification in the State of Odisha in particular and outside areas in general to ensure better quality organic products to the consumers.

The aim of OSOCA, inter alia, include the following:

  • Provivde Organic Certification at a resonable cost.

  • establish a globally competitive certificate programme.

  • committed to maintain accrediation.

  • make available quality organic products through organic certification

Steps For Certification

Request for Organic Certification

Forwarding of necessary formats by OSOCA

Submission of application for Organic Certification

Scrutiny of application & approval of Organic plan by OSOCA

Contract between the operator & OSOCA.

Inspection of Organic projects by the inspectors of OSOCA

Certification decision by OSOCA

Issue of certificate & label approval by OSOCA

Issue of Transaction Certificate to eligible operators for processing & trading.
